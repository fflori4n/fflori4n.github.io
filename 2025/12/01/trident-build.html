Self-source Voron Trident 250 build - in pictures

Dec 1, 2025

This self source Voron Trident build was cobbled together from an old Anet A8 (actually my first printer back from 2016) and my Ender 3V2, sacrificing these two, the aim was to transplant each printer’s machine spirit to get a hybrid machine that is fiery, indestructible and just won’t quit like an Anet A8, but also infinitely moddable, (fast AND cheap AND good) with questionable design choices like an Ender 3.

The main features are the tri-gantry bed, the hallmark of the trident; Cool mainboard and toolhead that use CAN, Stylish industrial looking front panel with tactile switches and “build in” electrostatic air scrubber based on an IKEA förnuftig.

The toolhead uses parts from a Creality Sprite Pro + an EBB42 CAN board - I call it the Spriteburner - this is only V1 and might get it’s very own blog post, still thinking how to add a filament cutter into the mix.

Since this project is mostly based on the trident itself, I will not give too many details, just some pictures of the build.

!All parts of this build are acquired legally, do not replicate anything seen on pictures, danger of fire and electrocution and more fire, and a little bit of finger pinching!
